Catalogue of Audio Guides to the Birds of the Americas / Catálogo de Guías Sonoras para las Aves de América

Version 1

Bernabé López Lanús & Viviana Caro

 

 

CD-ROM with a painstakingly prepared and very extensive spreadsheet, allowing the user to easily find out on which CDs, cassette tapes, LP records, or CD-ROMs a bird's voice can be found. Click on the pictures below to see the full-size screenshots, so as to get a better impression of the invaluable tool offered by this CD-ROM. A Spanish language version of the spreadsheet is also presented.

From the back cover: "The 103 titles analysed gave a total of 3147 bird species represented by more than 12,300 tracks.  (..) Our idea is to create awareness of these products even if they can not be easily obtained either through their purchase or their copying, in the case of out of print titles. Surprisingly, many university and biology faculty libraries do not have these recordings. We hope to highlight this deficiency through the present database. This project will also be useful in deciding on priority species to release on new audio guides. The criteria of a subspecies not represented on any recording can thus take precedence over simply choosing recordings for their optimum quality. (..)"

CD-ROM for Windows. Published 2001 by Literature of Latin America (L.O.L.A.), Buenos Aires, Argentina.
