Bird Songs International

We published several DVD- and CD-ROMs about the birds of tropical places. Their emphasis is on bird voices, with photographs thrown in as well. They are not field guides!

Our publications:

  • "Birds of Ecuador / Aves de Ecuador", DVD-ROM by Niels Krabbe and Jonas Nilsson
  • "Birds of Bolivia / Aves de Bolivia 2", CD-ROM by Sjoerd Mayer
  • "Birds of Venezuela / Aves de Venezuela", CD-ROM by Peter Boesman
  • "Birds of Tropical Asia 3", DVD-ROM by Jelle Scharringa

Updated/corrected/improved programs are available for: Ecuador (click here for English, clicquee aqui para español), Bolivia (click here for English, clicquee aqui para español), and Tropical Asia 3 (click here).
