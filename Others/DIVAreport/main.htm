Recent Ornithological surveys in the Valles region, southern Bolivia

and possible role of Valles for the evolution of the Andean avifauna

Jon Fjeldså and Sjoerd Mayer

 

This report contains an initial survey of the bird fauna in previously unexplored forests in the sub-Andean zone of southern Bolivia, and an analysis of avian biogeographic patterns within the region.

Published by the Centre for Research on the Cultural and Biological Diversity of Andean Rainforests (DIVA).
