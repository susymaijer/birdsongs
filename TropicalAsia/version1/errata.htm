Errata of the CD-ROM "Birds of Tropical Asia 1"

by Jelle Scharringa

  • Philippine Serpent-Eagle Spilornis holospilus: both photographs are actually of Oriental Honey-Buzzard Pernis ptilorhyncus
  • Yellow-breasted Fruit-Dove Ptilinopus occipitalis: the second and third recordings actually belong to Black-chinned Fruit-Dove Ptilinopus leclancheri. This was discovered by Pete Morris.
  • Coral-billed Scimitar-Babbler Pomatorhinus ferruginosus: the scientific name on the CD-ROM is wrong.
  • send e-mail to Jelle Scharringa if you think you found an error not listed above.
