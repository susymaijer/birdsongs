Errata of the CD-ROM "Birds of Tropical Asia 2.0"

by Jelle Scharringa

  • The flying bird on the photo of Black Baza Aviceda leuphotes is actually an Oriental Honey-Buzzard Pernis ptilorhyncus. Erratum added in August 2003.
  • The bird on the photo of a flying Steppe Eagle Aquila nipalensis is actually a Greater Spotted Eagle Aquila clanga. August 2003.
  • The third recording of the Collared Kingfisher Halcyon chloris (by Kees Scharringa) is not the call of this species, but the call of Stork-billed Kingfisher Pelargopsis capensis, as pointed out by Philip Round. April 2002.
  • The first recording of White-browed Shortwing Brachypteryx montana (from Sumatra) is not of this species but is the song of the Blue Whistling Thrush Myophonus caeruleus. Discovered by Antero Topp antero.topp@alula.fi. April 2004.
  • The first recording of the Common Tailorbird Orthotomus sutorius is not the song of this species, but the song of the Little Spiderhunter Arachnothera longirostra, as pointed out by Philip Round. June 2002.
  • The second call of the Ashy-throated Leaf-Warbler Phylloscopus maculipennis belongs to the Buff-barred Leaf-Warbler Phylloscopus pulcher and not to the first species. Discovered by Magnus Robb and Jelle Scharringa. February 2002.
  • The second photo of the Scaly-crowned Babbler Malacopteron cinereum is not of this species but of the Rufous-crowned Babbler Malacopteron magnum. Pointed out by Andrew Colthorpe in November 2004.
  • Both recordings of the Limestone Wren-Babbler Napothera crispifrons actually are the song and calls of the Streaked Wren-Babbler Napothera brevicaudata. This was discovered by Philip Round. June 2003.
  • The recording of the Rufous-backed Sibia Heterophasia annectens is not the call of this species, but the song of the Chestnut Bulbul Hypsipetes castanonotus, as pointed out by Philip Round. April 2002.
  • The photo of the Bay-backed Shrike Lanius vittatus is not of this species but of the Long-tailed Shrike Lanius schach of the subspecies erythronotus. August 2003.

send e-mail to Jelle Scharringa if you think you found an error not listed above.
