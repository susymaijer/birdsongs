Play your North American bird and frog CDs the easy way!



I'm currently busy making a program which both shows and plays the contents of commercially available (audio) CDs. See the screenshot to get an idea of what it does.

The following CDs are included at the moment:

  • Bird Songs of Alaska, Leonard J. Peyton
  • Bird Songs of the Pacific States, Thomas G. Sander
  • Bird Songs of the Rocky Mountain States and Provinces, Robert Righter and Geoffrey A. Keller
  • Bird Songs of the Lower Rio Grande Valley and Southwestern Texas, Geoffrey A. Keller
  • Bird Songs of Florida, Geoffrey A. Keller
  • Guide to Bird Sounds, for use with the National Geographic Society's Field Guide to the Birds of North America
